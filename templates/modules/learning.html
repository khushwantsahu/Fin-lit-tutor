🧠 Quick Knowledge Quiz

Q: To receive money on UPI, you should:

A) Enter your UPI PIN when requested
B) Just share your UPI ID or QR code — PIN is never required to receive
C) Share your bank account OTP
D) Click the payment request link sent by stranger
✅ Correct! You NEVER need a PIN to receive money on UPI. If someone asks for your PIN to send you money, it's a SCAM. Call 1930.
