⚠️ Common Scam Types in India

📱 UPI Scams
  • Requesting money to send you money (collect request fraud)
  • Fake merchant QR codes at shops
  • Phishing links pretending to be UPI apps
  • Impersonating family members in distress
🛡️ Safe Rule: You NEVER need to enter PIN to RECEIVE money. PIN is only for sending.
📞 Vishing / Phone Scams
  • Fake bank employee calls asking for OTP
  • KYC update urgency scams
  • Loan approval calls asking for processing fee
  • Income tax refund fraud calls
🛡️ Safe Rule: Banks will NEVER ask for OTP/PIN/CVV on calls. Hang up immediately.
💻 Online Investment Scams
  • "Double your money" schemes
  • Fake Telegram/WhatsApp investment groups
  • Unregistered stock market advisors
  • Ponzi/MLM financial schemes
🛡️ Safe Rule: Check SEBI registration at sebi.gov.in. No guaranteed investment returns exist.
🔄 SIM Swap Fraud
  • Criminal gets duplicate SIM for your number
  • Intercepts all OTPs and banking alerts
  • Drains your accounts within hours
  • Uses social engineering with telecom companies
🛡️ Safe Rule: If your SIM stops working suddenly, call bank immediately and block account.
